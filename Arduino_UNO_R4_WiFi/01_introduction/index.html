Chapter 1: Arduino UNO R4 WiFi

Section 1.1: Arduino UNO R4

Arduino UNO R4 comes into the market in late May 2023. Arduino UNO R4 has two types of boards:

  • Arduino UNO R4 Minima
  • Arduino UNO R4 WiFi

This tutorial will focus on Arduino UNO R4 WiFi.
